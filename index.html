About Golden

Jungkook’s debut solo album Golden was released in November 2023, marking a new chapter in his musical journey beyond BTS. The album reflects his artistry, growth, and versatility, combining pop, R&B, and EDM influences to create a unique sound that resonates globally. As the youngest BTS member, Jungkook demonstrates how far he has come, taking the spotlight confidently while maintaining his signature charm and authenticity.

The album highlights Jungkook’s passion for music and storytelling, with tracks that explore love, ambition, and personal growth. Each song offers a glimpse into his evolving identity as a solo artist, proving that he is more than capable of captivating audiences on his own. Golden also reflects his dedication to fans, often expressing gratitude and sharing his journey through meaningful lyrics and heartfelt performances.

Highlights of the Album

  • Released on November 3, 2023
  • Features international producers and artists
  • Blends multiple genres including pop and R&B
  • Became one of the best-selling K-pop solo albums
