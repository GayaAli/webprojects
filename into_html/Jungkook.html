About Jungkook

Jungkook is a member of the South Korean boy band BTS. He was born on September 1, 1997, in Busan, South Korea.

Why He is Famous

Jungkook is known for his singing, dancing, and stage performances. Fans call him the "Golden Maknae" because he is good at many things.
